\kafkasection{Four objects of mass $m$ are constrained to a circle of radius $R$. Each object is
connected to its two neighbors by a spring with spring constant $k$. The springs
are also confined to the circle making the motion basically one-dimensional with
periodic boundary conditions. Find the Lagrangian and derive the equations of
motion. Determine the eigenvalues and normal frequencies of the system and
discuss the corresponding normal modes.}
