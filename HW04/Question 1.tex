\kafkasection{Four objects of mass $m$ are constrained to a circle of radius $R$. Each object is
connected to its two neighbors by a spring with spring constant $k$. The springs
are also confined to the circle making the motion basically one-dimensional with
periodic boundary conditions. Find the Lagrangian and derive the equations of
motion. Determine the eigenvalues and normal frequencies of the system and
discuss the corresponding normal modes.}
Here we have a system with no external forces. The lagrangian is then:
\begin{equation}
    \Lagr = \sum T - \sum U
\end{equation}
For the kinetic energy, we see that the motion is bounded to a circle. The displacement of a given object is then:
\begin{equation}
    x = R\theta
\end{equation}
Where $\theta$ is the angle of displacement from the given object's equilibrium position. We then have the kinetic energy:
\begin{equation}
    T = \frac{1}{2}m R^2 \dot{\theta}^2
\end{equation}
The total kinetic energy is then:
\begin{equation}
    T = \frac{1}{2}m R^2\sum_{i=1}^{4} \dot{\theta}^2
\end{equation}
